Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Persona Lab ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the Dazza and Shazza applications ("the apps").

1. Information We Collect

When you use the apps, we collect:

  • Message content: The text messages you send within the app. These are sent to our backend server and forwarded to the Anthropic API to generate character responses.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information such as session activity, collected to understand how the apps are used and to improve performance.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information unless you create an account (if applicable).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate character responses via the Anthropic API
  • To maintain and improve the apps
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

We do not use your messages to train AI models. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The apps use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic: Your messages are sent to the Anthropic API to generate responses. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.
  • Railway: Our backend servers are hosted on Railway. Data in transit passes through Railway's infrastructure.

4. Data Retention

Message content is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of a single conversation session. We do not maintain conversation history between sessions.

5. Children's Privacy

The apps are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below.

6. Age-Gated Content

Some features of the apps may include adult content (17+ mode). Access to such features is restricted to users who are 17 years of age or older.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information transmitted through the apps. All communication between the apps and our backend servers uses HTTPS encryption.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
