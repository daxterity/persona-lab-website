Are Dazza and Shazza real people?

Nah — they're AI characters built by Persona Lab. But we reckon they're pretty bloody convincing.

Anything really — but they shine on Aussie sport, weather, lifestyle, and just having a yarn. Premium subscribers get live web search so they're across what's happening right now.

What's the difference between free and premium?

Free tier gets you the full character experience. Premium unlocks live web search, real time sport scores, weather, and news — plus no ads.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Through your Google Play account settings. Go to Play Store → Subscriptions → cancel anytime.

If you'd like to delete your account and data, head to personalab.com.au/delete-account and submit a request. We'll action it within 30 days.
